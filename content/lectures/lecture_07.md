## **`ESDA`**
#
## Recapitulation

**ESDA** is a family of techniques to explore and spatially interrogate data

Main function: characterize **spatial autocorrelation**, which
  can be explored:

* **Global**ly (e.g. Moran Plot, Moran's I)
* **Local**ly (e.g. LISAs)
